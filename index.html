About Us

Every production starts with a dream or an artistic vision.

Our passion is bringing those dreams and visions to life.

For more than 60 years Wild Circus has been realising dreams by staging some of the world’s most famous shows and most memorable productions in cities across the globe.

Wild Circus has a gilded history of success in providing entertainment in complex and challenging environments. We are able to operate successfully throughout the world as we pay particular attention to cultural traditions and have unparalleled experience of producing shows.

Wild Circus prides itself on delivering high-quality, artistic entertainment that makes audiences laugh and giggle, and gasp. Wild Circus believes performance art exists to push the boundaries of the human imagination, entertainment exists to bring family, friends and audiences together.

We know how to manage each step of the creative process so that an artistic vision can be brought to life. Our costumes are handmade. Our animals are trained by us. Our sets are painted in the barns on our farm. We burn the midnight oil to conjure new visions for the show. It's all we do.

Circus is our job, our life, our love. Come to Wild Circus and be a part of the magic!
